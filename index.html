Everything you need to know about handing your property over to a professional team — answered straight from our Owner's Handbook.

Owner disbursements are sent electronically each month once rent has been collected and cleared. You will also receive a detailed monthly owner statement covering all income and expenses for your property, and a full year-end report to simplify tax time.

All of this is accessible through your Owner Portal so you can review your financials at any time without waiting on a call or email.

Learn about rent collection & disbursement →

We pull a comprehensive background check on every adult applicant — covering credit history, eviction records, criminal history (including sex offenses), foreclosure and bankruptcy history, residential history, and employment verification. We use software equipped with identity verification technology and Red Flag compliance tools to catch fraud before a lease is ever signed.

If an applicant has a complex situation — multiple pets, a special request, or borderline qualifications — we contact you directly to discuss it before making a decision. You are never left out of a call that matters.

Learn about our full screening process →

We stay proactively in contact with tenants to address issues before they become defaults. When rent is late, we issue late fee assessments per the lease terms and begin our collection process immediately. If a tenant refuses to pay or cannot be brought current, we will recommend initiating an eviction and walk you through the legal process under Texas law.

Texas courts are landlord-friendly — nonpayment of rent is taken seriously by Justice of the Peace courts, and we can attend the initial hearing on your behalf.

Learn about our eviction process →

Tenants submit maintenance requests 24/7 through the Tenant Portal. Our management agreement includes a $1,000 authorization limit — meaning we can approve and dispatch vetted, licensed, insured vendors for repairs under that threshold without requiring your approval each time. This keeps your tenants happy and your property protected without constantly interrupting your life.

For repairs above that threshold (outside of emergencies), we contact you first. Emergency situations — burst pipes, HVAC failure in extreme weather, sewer backups — are handled immediately to protect the property and tenant safety.

Learn about our maintenance process →

You have 24/7 access to your Owner Portal — a secure online dashboard where you can view your property's financial statements, current tenant details, maintenance history, and inspection reports. Monthly owner statements are generated automatically, and your team is always available during business hours to answer questions.
