What You Receive

Full Visibility into Your Property's Performance

Every dollar that comes in and goes out of your property is tracked, categorized, and reported to you with full documentation. Whether you own one home or a growing portfolio, you have access to the same level of financial detail.

Monthly Owner Statement

A detailed accounting of all rent collected, management fees, maintenance expenses, and other charges — delivered each month alongside your electronic disbursement.

Year-End Report

A comprehensive annual summary of all income and expenses for the full calendar year — formatted to simplify your tax preparation and maximize your deduction documentation.

Vendor & Maintenance Receipts

Every repair and maintenance expense is documented with the original vendor invoice and available for download at any time.

Tenant Payments

A full record of charges assessed and payments received on your tenant's account — updated in real time as transactions are processed.

Owner Portal

Your Property at Your Fingertips — 24/7

Your Owner Portal is a secure, always-on dashboard giving you real-time visibility into everything happening at your property. No waiting for a callback. No digging through emails. Everything is in one place.

Financial Statements

View and download current and historical monthly statements, year-end reports, and disbursement summaries.

Owner Contributions

Owners can securely contribute funds to their property through their Owner Portal via eCheck (ACH), debit, credit.

Inspection Reports

Move-in, periodic, and move-out inspection reports with photos — available in your portal after each inspection is completed.

Document Storage

Property management agreements, income tax records, property move-in reports, and other key documents are stored securely in your portal for easy reference. Current lease agreements and addenda – all accessible at a glance.

Property Management Agreement

The latest version of the Property Management Agreement is always available in your portal so you stay current with policies and procedures.

Disbursements

Your Money, Delivered Electronically

Owner disbursements are sent electronically each month once rent has been collected and cleared. There are no checks to cash, no trips to the bank, and no delays. Your net proceeds — after management fees and any approved expenses — are transferred directly to your account with a full statement attached.

  • Electronic transfer direct to your designated bank account
  • Detailed statement accompanies every disbursement
  • All deductions are itemized with documentation
  • Historical disbursement records accessible in Owner Portal at any time

No prices are disclosed on this page. Management fee structures and disbursement timelines are outlined in your Property Management Agreement. Contact our team for a complete overview.
