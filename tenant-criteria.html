Tenant Screening

Tenant Selection
Criteria

Every adult 18 years or older who will reside in the property is required to submit their own application. Credit and criminal background reports will be completed for all adult applicants.

Apply & Pay Online → PetScreening →
Application Fee: $60 per adult 18 years or older when the original list rent is less than $1,900. $70 per adult 18 years or older when the original list rent is $1,900 or more. All application fees are non-refundable.

Government-Issued Photo Identification

A current, valid, government-issued photo ID is required from every applicant at the time of application.

Credit History

Credit approval will be based on the lowest credit score among all co-applicants.

Credit score under 580 may be declined.
Credit score between 580 and 600 requires an increased security deposit of 2× monthly rent.
Credit score of "N/A" requires an increased security deposit of 3× monthly rent.

Any co-signer must have a FICO score of 700 or above.

Criminal History

Any applicant convicted of a crime that demonstrates risk to person or property, and/or the illegal manufacture of, or distribution of, a controlled substance within the last 7 years will be declined.

Verifiable Current Gross Income

Combined gross monthly income for all financially responsible adult applicants must be at least 3 times the amount of the monthly rent for the property being applied for. Gross monthly income for any co-signer must be 4 times the amount of the monthly rent.

Each applicant must upload 2 months of their most recent paystubs, and/or:

  • If income comes from additional sources, upload award letters and/or Judgment(s) for each income source. You may also be asked to provide full and unaltered bank statements in PDF format for the previous 6 months to verify additional income.
  • All employment must be verifiable by name of employer, hire date, and gross monthly income.
  • If your employer uses a third-party verifier (such as The Work Number), you may: (a) furnish a printout from your company or the verifier showing your name, company, hire date and gross income; or (b) pay the cost of the verification in addition to your application fee. Verification cost will be not less than $100 by cashier's check or money order. Not paying this fee at the time your application is submitted may delay processing.

Self-Employed / IRS Form 1099:

  • Upload one or more previous year's tax returns attested by a CPA, Attorney, or other tax professional, and full and unaltered bank statements in PDF format for the previous 6 months.
  • If self-employment income is from an app-based company (UBER, Lyft, Postmates, etc.), provide full and unaltered pay statements showing name, company, date, and gross receipts on each document.
If an applicant is currently receiving unemployment benefits, the application will be denied unless sufficient income from other sources can be verified.

Rental History

Minimum of 2 recent years of verifiable, paid rental or mortgage history is required.

Less than 2 years of verifiable paid rental/mortgage history requires an increased security deposit of 2× monthly rent. Rental payments to family members will not be considered paid rental history.
Unpaid rental-related collection accounts and/or evictions within the last 5 years may automatically disqualify an applicant.

If you are currently in a residential lease, proof of payment in full to your current landlord may be required prior to approval.

Pets & Animals

PetScreening is a required part of the application process for all applicants — including residents and applicants who do not own a pet or animal.

All animals who will reside at the property must be registered at the time of application: c21allianceproperties.petscreening.com

  • Upload current rabies vaccination documentation for each pet/animal.
  • Upload 2 recent photos of each pet/animal.

Please note that not all properties allow pets. All requests to include pets will be at the owner's sole discretion.

A request for an assistance animal or support animal must be recommended by PetScreening within 5 days from the original submission date.

Move-In Costs

Payment Requirements

All move-in funds must be paid by cashier's check or money order only. Made payable to "Century 21 Alliance Properties" — brought in or overnight mailed to our office.

Security Deposit

Security deposit, at minimum, will be equal to one full month's rent.

Full security deposit + $200.00 admin fee must be paid in full within 24 hours of approval.

Due on or Before Lease Start Date

  • Full 1st month's rent (2nd month's rent will be prorated, if applicable)
  • Pet fees/deposits
  • Any additional rent/fees agreed upon by applicant/tenant and landlord per lease
